A Little Red, 3 Colors Trilogy

Bethany Maines invites readers into a world of supernatural intrigue and forbidden desires with her paranormal shifter romance, "A Little Red." The narrative centers around Scarlet Lucas, a headstrong woman who defies her family to pursue a career in the city. Little does she know that her path will intertwine with an otherworldly love affair, sparked while dressed as Little Red Riding Hood. The enigmatic Liam Grayson, an alpha male committed to combating evil, becomes Scarlet's irresistible boss, setting the stage for a romance that defies both societal norms and supernatural conventions. As Thanksgiving approaches, the story takes a compelling turn, delving into family dynamics, magical factions, and a world undergoing profound shifts. Scarlet finds herself at odds with her siblings, adding depth to her character, while readers gain insights into the intricate dynamics of wolf packs and the broader aims of magical factions within Grayson's family. The forbidden office romance between Scarlet and Liam introduces an element of power dynamics and steamy tension, creating a narrative rich in complexity. The plot thickens when warlocks attack, placing Grayson in jeopardy and forcing Scarlet to confront the challenges that come with her unconventional love. Bethany Maines skillfully explores themes of love, resilience, and self-discovery within the context of supernatural elements. Scarlet emerges as a genuinely strong female character, possessing intelligence and self-assuredness while willingly navigating the complexities of her relationship with Grayson. The story masterfully blends steamy romance with humor and mutual respect, offering a satisfying reading experience for fans of shifter romance and paranormal fiction. "A Little Red" is a fast-paced and spicy romance with lovable characters and an engaging storyline. As the first installment in a promising trilogy, it leaves readers eagerly anticipating further developments in Scarlet and Grayson's captivating journey. Bethany Maines has crafted a compelling narrative that not only satisfies the heart but also sparks the imagination, making "A Little Red" a must-read for enthusiasts of the genre.
